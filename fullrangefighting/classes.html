I offer a variety of class options to give you the best possible training experience. Each option caters to a different style, goal or preference for what you desire out of your training.

  • Private: Classes are taught in a one on one setting. This allows for my full attention to a student's performance and growth.
  • Semi-Private: Classes are taught in a two on one setting. This allows for the development of training partners for study outside the class while still maintaining the high level of my attention to each student's performance and growth.
  • Small Group: Classes are taught in a three (or more) on one setting. This allows for students who wish to study together with more than one other partner. This can also translate into a Workshop setting.

Seminars and workshops, geared towards specific or advanced techniques in a focused way, are offered by need.
