For over 20 years I have been certified, training and teaching students in the martial arts. I teach in Phases of study, based in my background of Jeet Kune Do/Jun Fan Kick Boxing, Filipino Martial Arts and Ground fighting.

  • Phase 1: Includes, but is not limited to, Kickboxing, Trapping, and Close Quarter Combat.
  • Phase 2: Includes, but is not limited to, Stick fighting, Knife fighting, and other categories of weapons drawing from the Filipino Martial Arts.
  • Phase 3: Is the study of Ground fighting which does include Brazilian Jiujitsu.

All classes are taught from my home gym which is fully equipped for everything needed for training (multiple bags, mitts, gloves, weapons, etc.).
